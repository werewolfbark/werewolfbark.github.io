商品紹介 This plastic model assembly kit reproduces the Paris Dakar specification of the 1/9 big scale Casiba Elephant 850. In 1987, Juert Oriole won the Honda Siri Nubouu until the end of the game. Total length of finish is 25.5 cm. The gas tank design ensures a large capacity that is unique to the Paridaka, and the large fairing to protect the rider, you can enjoy the unique finish. The 850cc demodromic air-cooled two-cylinder engine has been reproduced in detail including carburetors and other supplements. The unique shape of the frame that houses it is also precisely modeled The tires are made of hollow rubber that realistically reproduces the block pattern of tires unique to off-road models. The spoke wheel is also delicately represented to enhance the underfoot experience. The front and back suspension are movable after finishing. The markings have been reproduced by Eddie Orioli car number 100. This product is a plastic model that requires assembly and painting. Adhesives, tools, paints, etc., are required for assembly and painting.
